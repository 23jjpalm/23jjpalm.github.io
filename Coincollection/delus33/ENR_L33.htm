Description:

Abstract:

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) IFR Low Altitude Enroute Chart series is designed to meet the needs of users who require digital images of FAA Instument Flight Rules (IFR) Aeronautical Charts. These files are digitally produced versions of the printed IFR charts. They do not go through the lithographic printing process and as a result some differences in appearance from the printed chart version may exist. All aeronautical information remains the same as on the paper version. These digital versions are NOT georeferenced. Only the bounding coordinates for the main body of the chart are provided. These PDF files can be viewed using commonly available software.
